Welcome to Hut8 RC Racing

My name is Travis Eiler, I race 1:10th scale remote control cars at Tacoma R/C Raceway (TRCR). TRCR is a Hobby Shop with an indoor clay track. They club race every Tuesday, Friday, and Sunday. They have a National jConcepts race in the Spring, and host a Northwest Indoor series in the Winter. The track is approximately 100' by 50', and the layout changed every month or two. If you want to check out RC racing you can watch it live at www.tacomarcraceway.liverc.com and hit play.

This website was created to analyze my racing performance. Feel free to browse the site through the navigation bar at the top of the screen. Thank you for visiting.
