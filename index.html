Welcome to Hut8 RC Racing

My name is Travis Eiler, I study Computer Programming at Clover Park Technical College. This website is my JavaScript library research project.

I race 1:10th scale remote control cars at Tacoma R/C Raceway (TRCR). TRCR is an indoor clay track. They club race every Tuesday, Friday, and Sunday. They have a National jConcepts race in the Spring, and host a Northwest Indoor series in the Winter. The track is approximately 100' by 50', and the layout changed every month or two. If you want to check out RC racing you can watch it live at www.tacomarcraceway.liverc.com and hit play.

I am building this website to analyze my racing performance, and watch videos of my races.

Click on Racing Analytics if you would like to see my race data.

Click on Race Videos if you'd like to watch some of my races.
