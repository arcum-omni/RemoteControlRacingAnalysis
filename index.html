Welcome to Hut8 RC Racing

My name is Travis Eiler, I race 1:10th scale remote control cares at Tacoma R/C Raceway (TRCR). TRCR is an indoor clay track. It is approximately 100' by 50', and the layout changed every month or two. If you want to check out the track I race on go to www.tacomarcraceway.liverc.com and hit play.

I use this website to analyze my race results, and watch videos of my races.

Click on Racing Analytics if you would like to see my race data, or click on Race Videos if you'd like to watch one of my races.
